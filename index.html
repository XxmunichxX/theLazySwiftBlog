TIP OF THE DAY FOR THE SAVIOR iOS DEVELOPER:

KeyPath is a powerful yet often underestimated feature. Many developers use it inadvertently in their daily programming without fully realizing its potential and importance. KeyPath offers a type-safe, flexible, and elegant way to reference and manipulate type properties. It enables powerful access to nested properties, simplifies handling of generic data, and ensures safety and stability with instance independence and compile-time type checking. Go in peace, spread the word.
